Release notes

Changelog

What's new and improved in Sky Matrix. We ship steadily, most updates arrive automatically the next time you open the app.

New login & AI features
July 2026
  • NewPIN & biometric login. Set a PIN once after activation, then unlock with PIN or Face ID / fingerprint. Your licence code is never re-requested, including across updates.
  • NewRead with AI (beta). An AI OFP reader parses less-common flight-plan layouts and flags any fields it's unsure about.
  • NewAnnouncement Prep. Upload an announcement template (photo, PDF or text) and have it filled with this flight's data and your name, in your chosen language.
  • NewColour-coded, time-windowed NOTAMs, METAR/TAF and D-ATIS on the inflight Nav screen, with automatic fallback to OFP weather when a live source is unavailable.
  • ImprovedOfficial Flightradar24 aircraft lookup for more reliable type and route data.
  • ImprovedAdjustable warm-up / cool-down timers (default 5 minutes) on the Departure and Arrival cards.
  • FixedAircraft type occasionally misread from certain OFPs, the parser now validates against real aircraft designators.
Accounts, pricing & guide
July 2026
  • NewSelf-service trials and subscriptions from the welcome screen, with a reminder 7 days before expiry.
  • ImprovedSimplified plans: Monthly $25, 6-month $130, Yearly $250, with a 7-day money-back guarantee.
  • ImprovedRefreshed marketing site and an updated user guide covering the new login and pricing.
Foundation
Mid 2026
  • NewOFP briefing with weights, fuel, NOTAMs, weather, route and enroute data.
  • NewInflight display with internet UTC clock, timers, ETA and reminder banners; crew-rest planner; dangerous-goods reference; announcement guide.
  • NewOffline-capable install (PWA) and an Android app; device-locked licensing.
