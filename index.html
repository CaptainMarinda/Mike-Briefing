Precision engine

Real numbers, not scraped text

Most tools just dump the PDF's text on screen. Sky Matrix parses it: figures like ETOW and Taxi fuel are captured as discrete, validated numeric values, then cross-checked and flagged if anything looks off.

Discrete values

ETOW, Taxi, Trip, ZFW and the weight limits are read as real numbers you can compare and compute with, not a wall of copied text.

Cross-checked

ETOW vs MTOW and estimated landing weight vs MLW are compared automatically, with a clear caution the moment a limit would be exceeded.

Flagged when doubtful

A pre-render data check highlights missing or out-of-range fields, so a mis-read number is surfaced for you to verify, never shown silently.

Privacy Policy

Sky Matrix keeps your data on your device and uses outside services only where a feature genuinely needs them. Here's exactly what stays local, what leaves, and why.

What stays on your device

Your flight data lives only in your browser/app storage on your device: the OFP text you load and every field you type (times, weights, crew, notes); your PIN, stored only as a one‑way hash; your biometric enrolment, which stays in your device's secure hardware and is never sent to us; documents you add to the in‑app DOCS store; and your saved announcements. None of this is uploaded to any Sky Matrix server. Clearing the app's data removes it.

What leaves the device, and why

A few features need the internet and send the minimum data required: Licensing & trials — your Device ID and email are sent to our licensing service to issue or validate an access code; AI OFP reading & announcements — when you tap these, the relevant OFP or template text is sent to be processed and returned; Aircraft, weather & NOTAM lookups — a flight number/registration or airport code is sent to the relevant provider; Payments — handled entirely on the checkout provider's site; we never see or store your card details.

Security model

Licences are protected with an ECDSA P‑256 digital signature — only our server holds the private key, so codes can't be forged, and each is bound to one device. Your PIN is stored as a salted one‑way hash; biometric unlock uses the platform authenticator (WebAuthn) and never exposes your fingerprint/face to the app. The app is served only over HTTPS, and sensitive data is never placed in URLs. Honest caveat: any client‑side gate deters casual access but is not a guarantee against a determined attacker — use a strong PIN and keep your device secured.

Your choices

You can stop all outbound data by simply not using the online features — the core briefing works offline. To delete your data, clear the app's storage on your device. For a licence transfer or a data request, email admin@skymatrix.biz. Questions about privacy or security: support@skymatrix.biz.
