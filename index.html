Inflight Display no OFP loaded

Live flight progress. Takeoff time drives the rest window and the checklist reminders. Destination & trip auto-fill from the OFP.

Tap START at takeoff to capture the airborne time (uses the device clock). Destination resolves automatically from the OFP. Your saved documents are under the DOCS button in the clock bar.

GPS auto-capture stamps takeoff when ground speed first exceeds 100 kt, and landing when it drops below 100 kt. Needs location permission and a GPS fix (works on cellular/GPS iPads; Wi-Fi-only devices have no speed).
