What it does

Everything for the brief, nothing you don't need

Purpose-built for line pilots. Load your operational flight plan once and Sky Matrix organises the whole picture across the phases of flight.

OFP briefing

Drop in your operational flight plan and get route, weights, fuel, remarks and enroute data laid out and cross checked automatically.

Weather & NOTAMs

Departure, enroute and arrival weather alerts, NOTAMs and live airport lookups with D-ATIS, parsed and highlighted for quick scanning.

Inflight display

An internet-synced UTC clock, distance travelled, and the next waypoint compared to your OFP for fuel and ETA at a glance.

Crew rest planning

Plan and time inflight rest cleanly, with the numbers worked out for you so the flight deck stays fresh for arrival.

Checklists & DGR

An editable checklist tab plus dangerous goods reference at your fingertips, build the flow that suits how you work.

Cabin announcements

Generate clean, consistent passenger announcements drawn from the flight's own details, ready to read out.

Precision engine

Real numbers, not scraped text

Most tools just dump the PDF's text on screen. Sky Matrix parses it: figures like ETOW and Taxi fuel are captured as discrete, validated numeric values, then cross-checked and flagged if anything looks off.

Discrete values

ETOW, Taxi, Trip, ZFW and the weight limits are read as real numbers you can compare and compute with, not a wall of copied text.

Cross-checked

ETOW vs MTOW and estimated landing weight vs MLW are compared automatically, with a clear caution the moment a limit would be exceeded.

Flagged when doubtful

A pre-render data check highlights missing or out-of-range fields, so a mis-read number is surfaced for you to verify, never shown silently.
