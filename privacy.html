Privacy & Security

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Sky Matrix is built to keep your data on your device and to use outside services only where a feature genuinely needs them. This page explains what stays local, what leaves the device, the third parties involved, and how to verify the Android app before installing it.

What stays on your device

Your flight data lives only in your browser/app storage on your device:

  • The OFP text you load and every field you type (times, weights, crew, notes).
  • Your PIN, stored only as a one-way hash, and your biometric enrolment, which stays in your device's secure hardware and is never sent to us.
  • Documents you add to the in-app DOCS store and your saved announcements.

None of this is uploaded to any Sky Matrix server. Clearing the app's data removes it.

What leaves the device, and why

A few features need the internet and send the minimum data required:

  • Licensing & trials, your Device ID and email are sent to our licensing service to issue or validate an access code.
  • AI OFP reading & announcement fill, when you tap these, the relevant OFP text or announcement template is sent to our service to be processed and returned. It is not used to build a public profile of you.
  • Aircraft, weather & NOTAM lookups, a flight number/registration or airport code is sent to the relevant data provider to fetch results.
  • Payments, handled entirely on the checkout provider's site; we never see or store your card details.

Security model

  • Licences are protected with an ECDSA P-256 digital signature, only our server holds the private key, so codes can't be forged, and each is bound to one device.
  • Your PIN is stored as a salted one-way hash; biometric unlock uses the platform authenticator (WebAuthn) and never exposes your fingerprint/face to the app.
  • The app is served only over HTTPS. Sensitive data is never placed in URLs.
  • Honest caveat: any client-side gate deters casual access but is not a guarantee against a determined attacker. Use a strong PIN and keep your device secured.

Android APK, verify before you install

If you sideload the Android app, confirm the file is authentic by checking its SHA-256 checksum against the value below before installing.

File: skymatrix.apk
SHA-256:
b2c398a40c5a9b0f84052cca9e7dc9a856f027e6e59c59f58b6746310e405dd6

Windows: certutil -hashfile skymatrix.apk SHA256  ·  macOS/Linux: shasum -a 256 skymatrix.apk. The output must match exactly. This checksum is for the current published build; it changes whenever the app is rebuilt.

Only install the APK from our official Download link. Because it is sideloaded, Android will warn about "unknown sources", that is expected; the checksum above is how you confirm it hasn't been tampered with.

Your choices

You can stop all outbound data by simply not using the online features, the core briefing works offline. To delete your data, clear the app's storage on your device. For a licence transfer or account/data request, email admin@skymatrix.biz.
