Frequently asked questions

Quick answers on trials, licensing, offline use, reading your OFP, login and refunds. Still stuck? Email support@skymatrix.biz.

Getting started
What is Sky Matrix?
Sky Matrix is a flight-briefing assistant for pilots. It reads your Operational Flight Plan (OFP) and turns it into a clear inflight companion: weather and NOTAMs, an inflight display with timers and reminders, crew-rest planning, dangerous-goods reference and announcement help. It is for reference and individual use only, not for operational use, the official hard-copy OFP always governs the flight.
How does the free trial work?
Every device can start one free trial directly from the app's welcome screen, no payment details needed. Tap Start Trial, confirm your email, and your access code is issued to that device. The trial runs once per device; when it ends you can subscribe to keep using the app.
How do I install it on my iPad or phone?
Open skymatrix.biz/app.html in Safari (iPad/iPhone) or Chrome (Android), then use Share → Add to Home Screen. It installs as a full-screen app and works offline after the first load. Android users can alternatively install the signed APK from the Download section of the home page.
Licensing & login
Why is my licence locked to one device?
Each access code is cryptographically bound to the device that generated the Device ID, using a signature only our server can produce. This keeps licensing simple and fair, one licence, one device. If you replace your device, contact admin@skymatrix.biz to move your licence.
What is the PIN and biometric login?
After you activate the app you set a PIN once. From then on, each time you open the app you unlock it with that PIN, or with Face ID / fingerprint if you enabled biometric unlock. You are never asked to re-enter your licence code, and app updates never re-prompt for it.
I forgot my PIN, what now?
On the login screen choose Forgot PIN. Because your licence is still valid on the device, you can reset the PIN and set a new one without buying a new code. If you cleared the app's data entirely, you may need to re-activate with your existing access code.
What happens when my trial or subscription expires?
You'll see a reminder popup starting 7 days before expiry. When it lapses, the app shows a "get a new code" screen and returns to the welcome flow so you can subscribe or renew. Your typed flight data stays on the device.
Do app updates or reinstalling need a new code?
Updates: no. The app updates itself automatically, you simply get the latest version next time you open it, and your access code and PIN are never re-requested. Reinstalling on Android: also no, your licence is tied to a stable device identity that survives uninstall and reinstall, so the same code keeps working. On iPhone/iPad, clearing Safari's website data can reset the device identity; if that happens, email admin@skymatrix.biz and we'll re-issue your code for the new Device ID at no charge.
How do I update the app?
You don't have to do anything, feature and fix updates arrive automatically the next time you open Sky Matrix (it always loads the latest version). The only exception is a new Android APK shell: because it's installed outside the Play Store, occasional shell updates (rare) are announced on our changelog and you install the new APK manually.
Using the app
How do I load my OFP?
On the Load screen, drop in (or choose) your OFP as a PDF or text file, the app extracts the text and fills the briefing automatically. You can also tap Read with AI (beta) to have our OFP reader parse less-common layouts. If a field looks uncertain it is flagged for you to check rather than shown silently.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Once loaded, the app runs offline, timers, rest planning, the inflight display, your saved OFP and typed values all work with no connection. Features that fetch live data (PDF text extraction the first time, aircraft lookup, live weather/NOTAM refresh, AI reading and announcement fill) need internet.
How reliable is the aircraft and weather lookup?
Aircraft lookup uses the official Flightradar24 data service for type and route. Live weather and NOTAMs are pulled from public aviation sources; if a live source is unavailable, the app falls back to the weather already contained in your OFP. As with all data in the app, treat it as reference, verify against official sources.
What is the Announcement Prep tool?
In "My announcements" you can upload an announcement template (photo, PDF or text). The tool reads it, fills the blanks with this flight's data and your name in your chosen language, and gives you a finished script to copy. Always review before use.
Billing & privacy
What is your refund policy?
7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If Sky Matrix isn't for you, email admin@skymatrix.biz within 7 days of purchase and we'll refund you. Checkout and taxes are handled by Lemon Squeezy as merchant of record. When a refund is processed, the licence on that device is automatically revoked.
Do subscriptions renew automatically?
Yes, monthly, 6-month and yearly plans renew automatically at the end of each period so your access is uninterrupted. You can cancel any time before the next renewal from the billing link in your purchase receipt; access continues until the paid period ends.
What data do you collect?
Your flight data and settings stay on your device. We only process the minimum needed for licensing (your Device ID and email) and, when you use them, for AI OFP reading / announcements. Full details are on the Privacy & Security page.
