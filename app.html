2 — Flight tools

Crew roster, checklist maker, your PA scripts and admin documents now live under the Utilities tab.

Send feedback

Tell us what to improve or report a problem — it goes straight to the Sky Matrix team.

Checklists

Create multiple named checklists. Tap a tab to open it; use ‹ › to reorder. Saved on this device. Reset ticks before each flight.

Checklist maker

Turn any document into a checklist — PDF, Word, photo or text. Review the steps, then add them to your Checklist above.

Crew roster

Upload a crew roster — photo, PDF, Word or text. Sky Matrix AI reads the flight-deck and cabin names by rank (Captain / CP / Capt · FO / SFO / SO · CSD / CS / LCC / LC).

Dangerous Goods — Emergency Response

ICAO Emergency Response Guidance. Choose a hazard class and/or a drill letter from the menus to see the relevant response.

Announcement Maker

✎ Edit my saved details (name is fixed; position / airline / aircraft can change)

Name it Welcome to auto-show on the Departure page, or Descend for the Arrival page — both are auto-filled for the current flight. Any other name (Boarding, Delay, Diversion…) is saved for quick access.

Tap Sky Matrix AI on any saved announcement to expand it and fill it with the current flight's details.

Live flight progress. Takeoff time drives the rest window and the checklist reminders. Destination & trip auto-fill from the OFP.

Crew rest — from the Inflight Rest plan

GPS auto take-off & landing

Uses this device's own GPS (an iPad/iPhone's built-in GPS, or a paired Bluetooth receiver like a Garmin GLO if one is feeding iOS Location Services — whichever is available). Auto-stamps take-off at the first ground speed above 100 kt (then jumps to Live) and landing at the first reading below 100 kt after airborne (then jumps to Arrival), using internet UTC, and drives the NAV LOG. Needs location permission and a GPS fix.

Aircraft / flight lookup

Enter an ATC callsign (e.g. ABC123) for airline + route + live aircraft, or a registration (e.g. G-ABCD) for the exact type & operator. Most complete while the flight is airborne. Needs internet.
