1 — Load an OFP

Drop an OFP file here or click to choose  (.pdf or .txt)
PDF text is extracted in-browser via PDF.js (needs internet the first time). Your last OFP is remembered until you load another.
Tip: on Android you can open your OFP email, tap Share and choose Sky Matrix to load it straight in. On iPhone/iPad, open the PDF and use the file picker above.
Once your OFP is loaded, choose one: Sky Matrix Briefing (fast, works without internet, best on Qatar OFPs) or Sky Matrix AI (reads the OFP pages for the most accurate result across all airlines, needs internet).

Crew roster

Upload a crew roster — photo, PDF, Word or text. Sky Matrix AI reads the flight-deck and cabin names by rank (Captain / CP / Capt · FO / SFO / SO · CSD / CS / LCC / LC).

My announcements

Your own PA scripts / notes — saved on this device, available every flight.

Paste any announcement (or your own text), choose a language, and translate. Needs internet.

Upload an announcement template (photo/screenshot, PDF or text). Sky Matrix AI reads it, fills the blanks with this flight's data and your details, in your chosen language.

Send feedback

Tell us what to improve or report a problem — it goes straight to the Sky Matrix team.

Checklist

Your own checklist — add items, tick them off. Saved on this device. Use Reset ticks before each flight.

Dangerous Goods — Emergency Response

ICAO Emergency Response Guidance. Choose a hazard class and/or a drill letter from the menus to see the relevant response.

Inflight Display no OFP loaded

Live flight progress. Takeoff time drives the rest window and the checklist reminders. Destination & trip auto-fill from the OFP.

Tap START at takeoff to capture the airborne time (uses internet UTC). Destination resolves automatically from the OFP. Your saved documents are under the DOCS button in the clock bar.

GPS auto take-off & landing

Auto-stamps take-off at the first ground speed above 100 kt (then jumps to Live) and landing at the first reading below 100 kt after airborne (then jumps to Arrival), using internet UTC. Needs location permission and a GPS fix.

Aircraft / flight lookup

Enter an ATC callsign (e.g. ABC123) for airline + route + live aircraft, or a registration (e.g. G-ABCD) for the exact type & operator. Most complete while the flight is airborne. Needs internet.
